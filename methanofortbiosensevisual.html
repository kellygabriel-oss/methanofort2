WHAT TO LOOK OUT FOR

Warning signs that indicate declining water quality

🐟💨
Surface Gasping
⚡ LOW OXYGEN
Fish clustering at the surface, mouths opening rapidly. Indicates dangerously low dissolved oxygen — often caused by organic pollution.
🐠🌀
Erratic Swimming
⚡ CHEMICAL SHOCK
Frantic, spinning, or uncoordinated movement. Indicates sudden chemical exposure — pH crash, heavy metal spike, or toxic runoff event.
🌿🟡
Yellowing Plants
⚠️ NUTRIENT STRESS
Leaves turning yellow or pale. Indicates nitrogen toxicity or pH imbalance affecting plant root absorption — early HAB warning sign.
🟢🚨
Algae Overgrowth
⚠️ HAB WARNING
Thick green or blue-green surface layer forming. Indicates excess nitrogen and phosphorus — direct precursor to a Harmful Algal Bloom event.
🐟😔
Fish Isolating
⚡ DISEASE / STRESS
Fish separating from the school, hiding near the bottom or edges. Indicates disease outbreak, parasite infection, or sustained water quality stress.
🌱🟫
Brown/Rotting Roots
⚠️ pH IMBALANCE
Plant roots darkening and decaying instead of showing healthy white growth. Indicates bacterial infection or sustained pH outside the safe 6.5–8.5 range.
